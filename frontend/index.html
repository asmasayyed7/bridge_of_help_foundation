About Us

Bridge of Help Foundation supports underprivileged children and families through food and nutrition programs, volunteer activities, and events that help children feel safe and cared for. We also guide families toward trusted organizations for health and welfare support. Our mission is to empower communities through meaningful, sustainable work that creates real change.

Our activities

Each activity is carefully designed with local communities to create sustainable, long-lasting impact that addresses real needs.

Nutrition Care
Food & Nutrition Programs
We provide nutritious meals to children and families in need, ensuring no one goes hungry. Our programs focus on balanced diets, regular meal distribution, and spreading awareness about healthy eating habits for better growth and well-being.
Our food and nutrition programs deliver balanced meals to underprivileged families in partnership with local suppliers. We run weekly drives that directly reach hundreds of households, providing not just food but also nutritional education and hygiene awareness to create lasting healthy habits.
Health Safety
Healthcare & Medical Support
We offer access to basic healthcare services, including regular check-ups, medical camps, and emergency support. Our goal is to ensure every child receives proper care, promoting a healthier and safer life for underserved communities.
We partner with local hospitals and medical NGOs to run free health camps and check-up drives. Every child and family member receives a medical assessment, prescribed medicines, and follow-up guidance. Trained volunteers assist doctors and ensure the most vulnerable receive priority care and emergency support.
Education Future
Learning & Education Support
We empower children through education by providing school supplies, learning programs, and guidance. Our initiative helps build strong foundations, encouraging children to grow, learn, and create a brighter future.
We organise community engagement events that bring children together for sports, arts, and learning. These events strengthen social bonds, boost confidence, and give children safe spaces to express themselves, supported by trained volunteers and dedicated staff from Bridge of Help Foundation.
