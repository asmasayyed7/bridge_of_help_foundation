About Us

Bridge of Help Foundation supports underprivileged children and families through food and nutrition programs, volunteer activities, and events that help children feel safe and cared for. We also guide families toward trusted organizations for health and welfare support. Our mission is to empower communities through meaningful, sustainable work that creates real change.

Our activities

Each activity is carefully designed with local communities to create sustainable, long-lasting impact that addresses real needs.

Volunteer Opportunities

Choose opportunities that match your availability and skills.

Field Volunteer

⏱ 3–6 months
🌍 Various locations

Work directly with communities on our active projects. Ideal for those who want hands-on field experience.

Requirements:

  • Passion for social impact
  • Flexibility and adaptability
  • Willingness to learn

Remote Support

⏱ Flexible hours
🏠 Work from home

Support our operations remotely through marketing, communications, data analysis, or fundraising.

Requirements:

  • Reliable internet connection
  • Relevant professional skills
  • Minimum 3 hours per week

Local Ambassador

⏱ 2–4 hours / week
📍 Your city

Organize local fundraising events, awareness campaigns, and community engagement activities.

Requirements:

  • Strong communication skills
  • Event planning experience
  • Local network connections
